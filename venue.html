Venue and Perth Information

Venue

LEW 2026 will be hosted by the University of Western Australia (UWA) Business School. The specific room and on-campus directions will be confirmed closer to the workshop.

UWA Crawley campus (main campus): 35 Stirling Highway, Crawley WA 6009.

Travel and accommodation

Getting to Perth

Perth Airport (PER) is the main gateway to Western Australia. The CBD is roughly 20–30 minutes away by taxi or rideshare, depending on traffic.

  • Taxi / rideshare: straightforward for hotel and campus drop-offs.
  • Public transport: Transperth rail services connect the airport precinct to the city; from the CBD, frequent bus routes serve the UWA area.
  • Time zone: Perth operates on Australian Western Standard Time (AWST).

Getting around

  • Transperth: trains and buses cover the CBD, inner suburbs, and the UWA precinct.
  • CBD CAT buses: free services within central Perth (useful if you are staying in the CBD).
  • Walking: the CBD, Elizabeth Quay and Kings Park are easily walkable from many central hotels.

Where to stay

If you want to be close to campus, look around Crawley, Nedlands, Subiaco, West Perth and the western edge of the CBD. If you prefer restaurants and evening options, the CBD is convenient and well connected.

Examples near UWA / inner west

  • Wonil Hotel Perth (Nedlands)
  • Vibe Hotel Subiaco
  • Quest-style serviced apartments in West Perth / Kings Park area

Examples in the CBD

  • Duxton Hotel Perth
  • The Ritz-Carlton, Perth
  • Nesuto Mounts Bay Apartment Hotel (near Kings Park)

Booking early is recommended, especially if you plan to extend your stay or travel during school holidays.

Perth suggestions

  • Kings Park: one of the world’s largest inner-city parks, with excellent views over the Swan River and city skyline.
  • Elizabeth Quay & the Swan River foreshore: easy evening walk and dining options.
  • Fremantle: a popular day trip for heritage streets, cafes, and the harbour.
  • Rottnest Island: beaches and cycling; book ferries in advance if you plan to go.
